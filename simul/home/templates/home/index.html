About

Simul is a one stop solution to collaborating efficiently with your classmates. From attendance, submissions and timetable updates, stay in sync and effortlessly communicate with your peers. Log in and get access to the multitude of resources and tools aimed to make your college life a breeze!

Synchronized Reminders

Never forget an assignment or event again! Simul lets you create and synch reminders so you always show up on time to hand in that paper, or to get lunch with your classmates.

Timetable Management

Constantly confused by timetable updates and substitutions? Simul handles it for you with built in synchronized Timetables. Never miss a whatsapp message again!

Attendance Tracker

Lost track of how many classes weren't worth getting out of bed at 8 am for? With Simul's new attendance tracker, bunk away stress free, at least until you aren't in the danger zone
